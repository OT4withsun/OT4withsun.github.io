Ep 001 - Origin Story of a New Moo

Published 2021-11-13

We’re here! It’s the FIRST episode of 4withsun Mamamoo podcast. And it’s a long one. We talk about Solar and her World is One global concert. We check up on Moonbyul and her 100th episode of Studio Moonnight. Wheein living her best life as an IG model. And I share all the angst and longing I had around Hwasa’s solo comeback. Then, during the Resonance Round I share my origin story as a new Moo. I tell a long long long looong story of how I became a stan of these wonderful and talented ladies.

Episode notes: 1) Hwasa released her Guilty Pleasure teaser literally a day after I had already recorded this episode. So no mention of the new teasers in this episode. We’ll discuss all of that next time. I’m excited!! 2) Search “K-POP Evolution” as a part of Youtube Originals for more information about the foundational influence of African-American Culture on the origins of K-POP music. 3) Also, I noticed the audio gets a little messed up towards the end of the episode. My apologies! Thanks for your patience. I’m still figuring stuff out.
