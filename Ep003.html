Ep 003 - Hwasa’s ‘Guilty Pleasure’ album is here!

Published 2021-11-22

I’m excited for this episode of 4withsun Mamamoo podcast. We’re going to continue our discussion of Hwasa’s 2nd solo album titled ‘Guilty Pleasure’ which dropped on November 24th. You’ll hear my commentary on the “I’m a B” music video (chart metrics, choreography, symbolism, etc). We’ll also discuss some of Hwasa’s appearances, including on Studio Moon Night with Moonbyul as well as Jessi’s Showterview. I absolutely loved EVERYTHING about my first comeback experience for Hwasa. It was truly wonderful. And I’m really proud of Ahn Hyejin.
