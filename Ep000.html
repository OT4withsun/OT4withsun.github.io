Ep 000 - Introducing “4withsun” Mamamoo Podcast

Published 2021-11-06

Hello and welcome! On this episode, I introduce my new podcast called “4withsun” which is focused solely on all things Mamamoo. I absolutely love OT4, and look forward to sharing info about Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein, and Hwasa one episode at a time.

We’ll discuss IG posts, twitter, MVs, comebacks, MMMTV, variety show appearances, solo activities, K-POP, and current events. I also hope to have a special “Resonance Round” sometimes where I take a deep dive into random topics related to the girls that I just can’t let go.

Join me as I openly stan and embarrass myself with how much I love this group. And sorry for all the singing (…t’s going to be a thing)! 2021-11-06 Email Contact: 4withsun@protonmail.com
